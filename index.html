About Me

I'm Rishika, a 17-year-old sub-junior powerlifter with a fierce passion for lifting heavy and chasing strength. I started my powerlifting journey in 2024, and within just one year, I've grown stronger, both mentally and physically.

What drives me? The pure love for lifting. Ever since I was a kid, I've dreamt of training in the gym, riding bikes, and living life with power—and now I'm turning that into reality, one lift at a time.
